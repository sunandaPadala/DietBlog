About

A healthy diet is one that helps to maintain or improve overall health.

Vision

“RightMyDiet's vision is to continuously make a positive difference in people's lives through diet and nutrition based informative interventions.”

Mission Statement

“Enable anyone seeking help with her / his diet plans, to easily find and access the required and correct information from competent sources, to be able to live healthy.”

Objective

  • Provide curated and accurate custom-diet plans for individuals, with advice from authorized and competent personnel.
  • Be the bridge between dietitians / nutritionists and health-seeking individuals, in order to facilitate seamless information exchange both ways.
  • Aggregate diet-based information from disparate and reliable sources, and make it easily accessible to the general public.
